Good collaboration fit

Where my research, methods and teaching experience can add value.

I am particularly interested in collaborations that connect rigorous quantitative research with practical analytical tools, research capacity development and evidence-informed decision-making.

Macroeconomics and policy

Inflation, monetary economics and macroeconomic dynamics

Research collaboration on inflation dynamics, monetary adjustment, interest rates, exchange rates, policy uncertainty, price transmission and related macroeconomic questions, particularly in Ghana and other developing and emerging economies.

Finance and financial systems

Financial economics, banking and market connectedness

Empirical research on financial stability, banking-sector soundness, commodity markets, financial markets, capital-market behaviour and the transmission of shocks across macroeconomic and financial systems.

Advanced quantitative methods

Econometrics, time-frequency analysis and causal methods

Collaboration on quantile-based methods, wavelet techniques, time-series and panel econometrics, TVP-VAR connectedness, non-parametric causality, Bayesian approaches, impact evaluation and advanced quantitative interpretation.

Data science and predictive research

Machine learning, explainability and reproducible analytics

Applied machine-learning projects involving predictive modelling, ensemble methods, model evaluation, SHAP-based explainability and the integration of data-science techniques into economic, financial and policy research.

Academic training and methods education

Postgraduate research training and quantitative capacity building

Workshops, short courses and academic training in R, Stata, EViews, SPSS, SmartPLS, NVivo, Excel, research design, data analysis, quantitative research methods, applied econometrics and reproducible research workflows.

Data literacy and institutional impact

Data capability, digital learning and evidence-informed programmes

Collaboration with universities, public institutions, educational programmes and development organisations on data literacy, professional analytical training, digital and flipped learning, mentorship and practical data use for organisational and community decision-making.
